Find Your Match

A curated speed dating event for singles.

I love love.. I think if you don’t you’re a pessimist and I consider myself an optimist.

One Friday evening my friend and I were at a bar talking about how hopeless dating apps were. Our mutual friend had just been catfished, others ghosted and so forth.

We thought about hosting a speed dating event where we would match people on multiple quick, 7 minute dates so people could meet each other and assess interest over a fun evening versus awkward first dates.

40+attendees
100+dates set up
25+matches

We ran multiple of these and the event was a success. We got over 40 attendees, set up 100+ dates, 25+ matches and one couple was still together last time I checked.

Don’t worry.. I used AI to create our very cute landing page and to help us with the matchmaking.
