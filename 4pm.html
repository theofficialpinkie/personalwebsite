4PM · CRM automation

Helping sellers get their 9-5 done by 4pm.

Working as a seller before I became a founder, I was always terrible at updating my notes and call details into Salesforce. It seemed simple enough for AI to solve this but also a real pain. I knew I hated it, and it took an hour per day.

We did things the right way here by pre-selling Ascend on the solution (see testimonial below). They paid before we built, which forced us to anchor the product in a real workflow instead of a pitch.

This helped us raise a pre-seed round of funding from investors including Daniel Gross of Pioneer Fund and Eleanor Dorfman, now Head of Growth at Anthropic.

We got many startups to start working with us including a venture capital firm who wanted a more simplistic call recorder, sales reps at high growth startups paying out of pocket for increased efficiency, and even sold an annual contract within three months of launching.

Building products I realize as creators we get lost in our own ideas. I've made the mistake of building what I felt and thought the market would want and now I don't like to spend too much time innovating solutions before I understand a buyer's mindset clearly.
